    ENERGY FOR INSTANT REACTION


    The Nike React Hyperdunk 2017 Flyknit Basketball Shoe features the latest revolution in basketball cushioning: ultra-responsive Nike React foam designed to help you play harder and go longer.


    Springy Response

    Ultralight, durable Nike React foam delivers a soft, springy sensation to help keep your feet comfortable through the final buzzer.


    Targeted Support

    Breathable Flyknit integrates areas of stretch and support where you need it most, conforming to the shape of your foot for a comfortable, lightweight feel. Flywire cables integrate with the laces for added support.


    Traction

    The rubber outsole supports your pressure points while delivering traction in exactly the right places.

    "The fit is really great so get the size you always get.
    The traction/grip is fantastic, I had zero slippage. Traction is very similar to Kyrie 2 as it wraps up the side of the shoe.
    My foot felt very stable and secure, it didn't move around at all. There's nice padding in the heels for comfort and lock down.
    The React cushioning.. I'm a little disappointed.
    It wasn't bad but I was expecting more. It's in-between court feel and soft comfort. It felt good under the forefoot but I felt a little more cushion was needed in the heel. I have to say I prefer full length zoom like last year's model.
    Experienced some pain around the ankle collar but I expect this to break in quickly. Nice materials used.
    Overall it's a good shoe for every position but I can't justify the 180Euro price tag.
    If you have a promotion code get them!!! If not I wouldn't be so sure if it's worth it."
    NikeEmea
