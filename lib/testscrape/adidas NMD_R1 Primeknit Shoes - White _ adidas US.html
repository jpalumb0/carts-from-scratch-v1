NMD_R1 Primeknit Shoes

Cutting-edge sneakers with a Japanese-influenced design.

Reflecting celebrated Japanese workmanship and detail, these men's shoes feature an engineered pattern mimicking Sashiko stitching on the one-piece adidas Primeknit upper. The bold running-inspired design is supported by a responsive boost™ midsole to bring energy and comfort to every step. Signature molded EVA plugs complete the NMD aesthetic.

  • boost™ is our most responsive cushioning ever: The more energy you give, the more you get
  • adidas Primeknit upper wraps the foot in adaptive support and ultralight comfort
  • One-piece upper with engineered pattern inspired by traditional Sashiko stitching
  • Molded EVA midsole plugs for the NMD aesthetic
  • Molded heel patch
  • Rubber outsole
  • Textile and synthetic upper / Textile lining / Rubber outsole
  • Imported
  • Product code: BZ0219
  • Product color: Running White / Running White / Core Black
